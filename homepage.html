Where to start?

When starting a homestead, it can feel overwhelming. It might even seem impossible to get started. This is why I created this website. I have compiled a list of 10 of the best homesteading websites I have come across and placed them in an easy to find list. I tried to give a few descriptors in the main focus section so you can check out the one that you might want to learn about first. Some homesteads focus on a single item like cooking, while others have a variety of features and topics that they cover. Just browsing their sites for a bit might just give you the inspiration that you need to get started. For me--getting started is the hardest part. So I encourage you to take the first step and check out one of the top 10 Homestead Websites on my list.
